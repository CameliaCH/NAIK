The Challenge

Why NAIK Exists

Millions of Malaysians are capable, talented, and hardworking — but lack the right information, support, and access to move up.

The Problem

Barriers that hold people back

  • 50% of Malaysian women are economically inactive, often due to family responsibilities
  • Many job seekers don't know their legal rights as workers
  • Information on real job opportunities is scattered or in English only
  • No affordable, accessible career coaching for low-income groups
  • Niche jobs exist but aren't visible to the people who need them
  • No "first-step" resource that works on low data / older phones
Our Solution

NAIK — Your all-in-one launchpad

Free, multilingual, mobile-first. Designed with and for the communities it serves.

  • Career quiz that matches you to real, reachable jobs in Malaysia
  • AI-powered tools to write your CV and practise interviews
  • Plain-language guide to your rights as a worker
  • A dedicated section for women re-entering the workforce
  • Scholarship and gig economy listings with deadline reminders
  • Runs on 3G — no data barrier
